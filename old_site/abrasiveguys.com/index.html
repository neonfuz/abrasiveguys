Founded in Milwaukee in 1963, J & R Industrial Sales Inc. became one of the largest distributors of abrasive products for Wisconsin's foundry industry. J & R has been a specialty distributor of abrasives from its inception and has never lost that focus. With the addition of Abrasiveguys.com, We now also serve the welding, steel fabrication, ornamental iron and construction industries Internationally.

Abrasiveguys.com distributes a wide variety of abrasive products that include large resinoid snagging and hot pressed cutoff wheels, as well as cup- and cone-type wheels. In addition to the resin-bonded lines, we also offer a full line of coated abrasives such as sanding belts, resin fiber discs, cartridge rolls, and surface conditioning products for grinding, blending and finishing.

With more than 50 years of combined experience in abrasives sales, the personnel at Abrasiveguys.com will provide you with the best products at the best prices and with the shortest lead times in the industry.

We search the world to find you the best abrasives!
