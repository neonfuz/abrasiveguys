Founded in Milwaukee in 1963, J & R Industrial Sales Inc. has become one of the largest distributors of abrasive products for Wisconsin's foundry industry. We also serve the welding, steel fabrication, ornamental iron and construction industries across the United States. J & R has been a specialty distributor of abrasives from its inception and has never lost that focus.

Through AbrasiveGuys.com, you can view J & R Industrial's product offerings, which shows a wide variety of abrasive products that include large resinoid snagging and hot pressed cutoff wheels, as well as cup- and cone-type wheels. In addition to the resin-bonded lines, we also offer a full line of coated abrasives such as sanding belts, resin fiber discs, cartridge rolls, and surface conditioning products for grinding, blending and finishing. Most recently we have added two new lines; cutting tools, and industrial bushes.

With more than 50 years of combined experience in abrasives sales, the personnel at J & R Industrial will provide you with the best products at the best prices and with the shortest lead times in the industry. Our knowledgeable staff is ready to assist you, please call or email us with any questions.
